The Watershed Warrior

Created by: Madison Gwinn

Sponsored by: FLOW

Work In Progress!

This is a choose your own adventure game where you will be presented with different scenarios and will be required to choose how to respond.

In this game you will play as Tim Burr, and fight against the evil Dr. Paul U. Shien to protect the Watershed. Progress through the story to unlock more powers, and play through multiple times to see every ending!

How To Play:


The battle feature runs on a turn based attacking/defending style of play. You will be able to choose which attack you would like to use, and then watch to see the outcome.


You will learn additional moves throughout the game, and hone your skills as you progress through the story
